About Me

Welcome! I’m Madison Kalivoda. To cut a long story short, I spent my early childhood in Colorado Springs, CO before moving to sunny San Diego, CA. In 2012, I moved back to Colorado to attend Colorado State University. I graduated from CSU with a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Studies and a minor in Business Administration. My love of the mountains, skiing, and the outdoors has driven me to call Colorado home.

I am currently a Full Stack Web Development student at the University of Denver. I have a passion for connecting people and businesses’ through creativity. I am fueled by my passion for communication, collaboration, and quest for knowledge. After graduating from the Full Stack Development Program, I would like to find myself in a career where I can merge my passions and my skill sets to connect businesses with their target markets through web and development efforts.
